WORLDVIEW

in which this is a page full of my messy thoughts that keeps changing depending on my mood.
just fyi: my religion does not relate to all of these thoughts.
these are not necessarily beliefs that i worship, but tho thoughts that i rely on currenlty.


how was the universe created?
i believe that the universe was created by a something - or someone.
perhaps it was God or maybe some sort of supernatural cause, i do not know.

what does it mean to be a human being?
everybody and everything has a purpose. there is no absolute randomness that you are placed in this earth for a random decision.
i believe that you have been placed in this world for a exact reason - for this exact time, at the exact location, as the exact person you are.
we all have a purpose. you do, i do. but i do not know what it is just yet.

what happens when you die?
perhaps you go to heaven - or hell?
i strongly do not believe that our soul will be "deleted" in history - or be reborn into the same world.
however, i believe that our soul will be ... restored. perhaps we'll go to paradise? heaven? hell? a second world?
i do not know.
